Why Join Tandooriwala?

At Tandooriwala, we believe in serving not just delicious food but also career growth. We're a fast-growing restaurant brand focused on innovation, team empowerment, and culinary excellence. Whether you're a chef, server, or aspiring manager, there's a place for you here.

Current Openings

  • Head Chef – 5+ years of experience in Indian cuisine.
  • Restaurant Manager – Strong leadership and hospitality background.
  • Wait Staff – Enthusiastic team players with good communication.
  • Kitchen Assistant – Energetic and quick learners welcome!

Our Work Culture

We value creativity, passion, and teamwork. Our kitchen is a space of both flavor and fun. We believe happy teams create happy customers, which is why we offer flexible shifts, training, performance bonuses, and career progression for all.

How to Apply

To join the Tandooriwala family, send your resume to careers@tandooriwala.in
Or visit your nearest branch and ask for the manager.
