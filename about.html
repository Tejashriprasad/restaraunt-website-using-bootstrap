About Tandooriwala

Location: 123 Spice Street, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

Area: 5000 sq.ft premium dining space with indoor & outdoor seating

Established: Since 2010, serving authentic Indian Tandoori cuisine

Tandooriwala is a destination for food lovers who enjoy the richness of traditional recipes blended with modern ambiance. Located in the heart of the city, we welcome you with warmth, flavors, and a memorable dining experience.
